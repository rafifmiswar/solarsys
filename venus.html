Venus is the hottest planet in our Solar System and the second brightest object in the world.



The estimated age of the Venusian surface is around 300-400 million years old compared to Earth which is about 100 million years old



Although this planet is famous for the opposite-direction rotation from other planets. This is known as a retrograde rotation and may have been caused by a collision with an asteroid or other object which caused the planet to change its rotational path.

DID YOU KNOW?

A day on the surface of Venus (solar day) would appear to take 117 Earth days.
